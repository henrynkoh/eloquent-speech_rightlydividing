4. **Bridge if Needed**: "That's a great question. What I can tell you is..."

**Crisis Communication Structure:**

- **Acknowledge**: What happened, be direct
- **Explain Briefly**: Context, not excuses
- **Action Plan**: What you're doing about it
- **Next Steps**: What happens next, timeline

## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Guidelines

- **Keep it Short**: 50 characters or less
- **Create Urgency**: "Start Today" or "Don't Miss Out"
- **Personalize**: Use [First Name] when possible
- **Clear Value**: What will they get?
- **Test**: A/B test different approaches

### Email Design

- **Mobile First**: 60%+ open on mobile
- **Clear CTAs**: One primary action button
- **Scannable**: Short paragraphs, bullets
- **Branded**: Consistent colors, fonts, logo
- **Plain Text Option**: Some prefer plain text

### Timing

- **Welcome**: Immediate upon signup
- **Daily Reminders**: Send at optimal time (test for your audience)
- **Follow-ups**: 30 days, 60 days, 90 days
- **Re-engagement**: If inactive for 7+ days

### Personalization

- Use [First Name] in subject and body
- Reference their progress ("Day 2" if on Day 2)
- Segment by completion status
- Customize based on behavior

### Compliance

- **Unsubscribe Link**: Required in all emails
- **Physical Address**: Required for CAN-SPAM
- **Clear Sender**: Use recognizable name/email
- **Permission**: Only email those who opted in
