## Performance Benchmarks

### Good Performance Indicators

- **CTR**: 1.5%+ (higher is better)
- **CPC**: $0.50-2.00 (varies by audience)
- **CPL**: $5-20 (if collecting leads)
- **Conversion Rate**: 10%+ (visitor to Day 1 start)

### Optimization Triggers

**Pause ad if**:
- CTR below 1% after 3 days
- CPC above $3.00 consistently
- No conversions after 100 clicks

**Scale ad if**:
- CTR above 2%
- CPC below $1.00
- Conversion rate above 15%
- Positive ROAS
