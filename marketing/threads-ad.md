```
Ritual-based communication: Following a script regardless of the audience. Sticking to the plan even when it's not working.

Spirit-led communication: Adapting to the moment. Listening before speaking. Reading the room and responding.

When you're led by the Spirit, you know when to speak and when to be silent.
```
